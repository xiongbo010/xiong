I am currently a doctoral researcher at the Analytic Computing Department, Institute for Parallel and Distributed Systems (IPVS), University of Stuttgart, Germany, where I was very fortunate to work with Prof. Steffen Staab. I am also an Early Stage Researcher (ESR) of European Marie Sklodowska Curie Initial Training Network (ITN) on KnowGraphs project.

Previously, I worked as a research intern & assistant with Dr. Zhenguo Li and Dr. Yimin Huang at Huawei Noah's Ark Lab, Dr. Jiang Bian and Dr. Jia Zhang at Microsoft Research Asia. Prof. Fei Qiao at Tsinghua University, Prof. Peng Bao at Beijing Jiaotong University (BJTU), respectively. Thank you very much for all of your valuable advising on my research.

Currently, my research interests lie mainly on representation learning for relational data, especially for knowledge graphs.
