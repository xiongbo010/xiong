Biography

I am a Ph.D. student at International Max Plank Research School for Intelligent Systems, as well as a Marie Sklodowska-Curie Early Stage Researcher at University of Stuttgart, Germany.

I am interested in developing mathmatically/geometrically inspired models for representation learning over relational data, e.g., knowledge graph embeddings, geometric representation learning, etc.
