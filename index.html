I am now a first-year PhD student working with Steffen Staab at the International Max Plank Research School for Intelligent Systems (IMPRS-IS) and the University of Stuttgart. In parallel, I am working as a Marie Skłodowska-Curie Ealry Stage Researcher (ESR) on KnowGraphs Initial Training Networks (ITN). Before that, I had wonderful internships at Huawei Noah's Ark Lab and Microsoft Research Asia.

Currently, my research interests lie mainly on machine learning and representation learning for relational data. In particulaly, I am interested in 1) knowledge graph representation; 2) geometric representation learning; 3) biological applications, e.g. drug discovery.
